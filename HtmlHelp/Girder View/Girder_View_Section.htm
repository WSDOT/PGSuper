View Settings - Girder Model View
Section View

Customize the girder section view by enabling or disabling the various view attributes.

Show Strands

When checked, the prestressing strands are shown

Show Longitudinal Reinforcement

When checked, the longitudinal reinforcement is shown

Show C.G. of Prestress Force

When checked, the center of gravity of the prestressing force is show with a red and white target symbol.

Show Dimensions

When checked, the overall girder dimensions are shown, as well as the location of the center of gravity of the prestressing force.
