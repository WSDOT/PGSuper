Favorite Reports

Favorite reports help you work more efficiently by removing clutter  from PGSuper's user interface.

To use favorites, first select your favorite reports by going to "Options | Reporting" from the main PGSuper menu. Then click on the reports you want to be favorites.

PGSuper will then only display Favorites in the "View | Reports" menu and the menu displayed from the icon on the toolbar. You can toggle both of these menus to display only favorites, or to display all reports by clicking the top item on the menu.

When you right-click on a girder in PGSuper, a context-sensitive menu is shown. This context menu will only display favorite reports.

Tip: You can create your own Custom Reports and make them your favorites.
