Custom Reports

Custom reports allow you to select and reorder a list of chapters from one of PGSuper's built-in reports, and then save it as your own report. Once created, custom reports are used in the exact same manner as built-in PGSuper reports. You can also share your custom reports with other users or move them to another computer.

To use custom reports, first create your custom reports by going to "Options | Reporting" from the main PGSuper menu. Then select the Custom Reports tab.

The list box shows a list of all custom reports on your system. From there, you can add new reports, edit existing reports, and delete reports from your system.

Tip: Custom reports can be put into your Favorite Reports list. This way you have quick access to frequently used custom reports.

Editing a Custom Report

To edit a custom report, double-click on the report in the list, or select the report and click the Edit button. Here, you have several options for defining your custom report.

Report Name

Enter a name for your custom report. Note that this name cannot be the same as other reports on the system including built-in reports and other custom reports.

Use Chapters from Parent

Select the parent for this custom report. A custom report may contain chapters only from its parent.  A custom report may only have one parent report.

Selecting Chapters

Upon selecting a parent, you will be presented with a list of available chapters that can be placed in your custom report. Use the ">>>>>>" and "<<<<<<" buttons to add and remove chapters from your custom report. Once selected, a chapter may be moved up or down to establish its location in the report.

Include this Report in Favorites

Check this option if you want the current custom report to be include in the favorites list. More information about favorite reports may be found here

Importing and Exporting Custom Reports

Custom report information may be saved to a file (exported) so that it may be shared with other PGSuper users. To export your custom reports, click export, select the reports that are to be exported, and then select the file where the report information is to be stored.

Once exported, you can import the report information by selecting the Import button and then the file to be imported. Note that imported reports may not have the same name as existing reports. If this condition exists, you will be prompted to rename or skip importing of the report.
