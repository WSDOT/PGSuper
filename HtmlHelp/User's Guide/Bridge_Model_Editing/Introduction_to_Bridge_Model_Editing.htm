Chapter 3 - Bridge Model Editing

In this chapter you will learn about the Bridge Model View, and how to edit your bridge model.

You can think of your bridge model at two different levels. At the top level, there is the overall configuration of the bridge, including the roadway alignment information, pier locations, girder framing, and the cross section. At the lower level, there is the details of each girder in the structure. This chapter will focus on modeling the bridge, the next chapter will focus on modeling individual girders.

All of the Bridge Model Editing is done in the Bridge Description Dialog. Open the Bridge Description Dialog by choosing Project | Edit Bridge (ALT,E,B ).

We'll first take a look at the Bridge Model View, which provides a visual representation of the bridge model. Then we'll dive into the details of bridge modeling.
