Structural Analysis Method

PGSuper has three structural analysis methods. They are:

  • Simple Spans

  • Simple Spans made Continuous

  • Envelope of Simple Spans and Simple Spans made Continuous

Select Project | Structural Analysis Method to set the analysis method type. The selected analysis method is always shown in the status bar.

All spans are treated as simple spans regardless of boundary condition when the Simple Spans analysis method is selected.

Simple spans are made continuous, using the connection boundary conditions defined at the piers, when the Simple Spans made Continuous analysis method is select.

If the Envelope of Simple Spans and Simple Spans made Continuous method is selected, both simple span and simple made continuous analysis are performed and the envelope, or controlling values, from these analyzes are used.
