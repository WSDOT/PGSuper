Strand Stresses Tab

This tab is used to specify the allowable stresses in the prestressing strands (LRFD 5.9.3). Values for both stress relieved and low relaxation strands must be specified.

Check at Jacking/Immediately Prior to Transfer

Strands stresses must be checked either at the time of jacking, or immediately prior to transfer (Release). Enter a fraction of the ultimate strand stress (fpu) that is allowable.

Check After Transfer

Strand stresses can optionally be checked immediately after transfer.  Enter a fraction of the ultimate strand stress (fpu) that is allowable.

After All Losses

Entry the fraction of the strand yield stress that is allowable in the final condition.
