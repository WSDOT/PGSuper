Bridge Site 1 Tab

Use this tab to define criteria pertinent at bridge site stage 1. Stage 1 is just after the slab and diaphragms have been poured. Slab material has weight, but no strength (the girder is noncomposite).

Allowable Concrete Stresses

Compressive Stress

Enter the multiplier to determine the maximum allowable concrete compressive stress for Service I limit state loads at Bridge Site Stage 1. Refer to Section 5.9.4.1.1 in the AASHTO LRFD Specifications for the recommended value.

Tensile Stress

Enter the multiplier to determine the maximum allowable concrete tensile stress for Service I limit state loads at Bridge Site Stage 1. Refer to Section 5.9.4.1.2 in the AASHTO LRFD Specifications for the recommended value.
