Standard Loading

PGSuper automatically generates all of the standard loading for a precast-prestressed girder bridge. These loading conditions include:

  • Self-weight dead load of the girders, deck, diaphragms, and the bridge railings

  • HL93 Live Load

  • Fatigue Live Load (AASHTO LRFD 2009 and later)

  • Pedestrian live load on Sidewalk

  • Overlays

Dead loads are based on cross sectional area of the members and the material unit weight. See the Structural Analysis Models section of the Theoretical Manual for a detailed explanation of how PGSuper builds analysis models and applies loads.

A construction load allowance that will be carried by the non-composite girder section can also be defined. This allowance covers additional loads incurred by the use of stay-in-place metal forms or other permanent construction materials and tolerances. This loading is applied to the entire bridge deck and is distributed to each girder based on its tributary area. This loading is applied to the DC load case.
