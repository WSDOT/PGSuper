Chapter 6 - Status Center

The PGSuper status center provides feedback about possible problems with the current state of your bridge model and analysis results. When errors occur or questionable input exists, PGSuper posts items to the status center for your review. For example, if your bridge configuration is such that it does not conform to the LRFD simplified method of analysis, an error occurs and detailed information is posted to the status center. You can open the status center to read the details and you will be offered suggestions on how to resolve the issue.

Another reason an item might be posted to the status center is for a user defined load that has "fallen" off the bridge. Let's say you put a concentrated load 125ft from the left end of your girder. Then you change the pier stations such that the girder is now only 120ft long. The load has fallen off the girder and will not be used in analysis. A warning will be posted to the status center to alert you to this situation. You can choose to ignore the problem, fix it using one of the suggested remedies, or fix it using your own method.
