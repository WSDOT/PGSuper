Lifting and Shipping

The final step in describing a girder is to specify where the lifting loops are to be placed and where the girder will be supported during transportation.

Lifting loops are placed equal distance from the ends of the girder. During transportation, the overhangs can be unequal. The Leading overhang is the one nearest the front of the truck. This distance is compare to the overhang limit criteria (If the overhang is too long, it will hit the cab of the truck).
