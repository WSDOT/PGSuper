Alignment Description - Superelevations

Superelevations

The cross section of the roadway/bridge deck is defined by a super elevation data. For each cross section change along the alignment, enter the station, left slope, right slope, and crown point offset.

Use negative values for slopes that are downwards from the crown point.

The crown point may be offset from the alignment with the crown point offset parameter. A positive offset locates the crown point to the right of the alignment.
