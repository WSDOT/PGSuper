Generalized Resistance Factor for Flexure

PGSuper generalizes the resistance factor defined in AASHTO LRFD 5.5.4.2.1. The resistance factor is a linear interpolation between the compression controlled and tension controlled sections. A linear interpolation is also made for partial prestress ratio between reinforced concrete and prestressed sections.

This article illustrates the derivation of the generalized resistance factor for flexure equation.
