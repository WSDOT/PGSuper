SUBJECT: Design Policy Memo - Shear Design

 

The following modifications shall be applied to the shear design provisions of the AASHTO-LRFD Bridge Design Specifications article 5.8.3.4.2:

1. The numerator of equations 1, 2 and 3 shall be changed to:

  Mu/dv + 0.5Nu + (Vu-Vp) - ApsFpo

This equation was changed to  |Mu|/dv + 0.5Nu + |Vu-Vp| - ApsFpo when AASHTO introduced the absolute values in their equations.

2. The flow chart of figure C5.8.3.4.2-5 shall be replaced with the flow chart in the attachment.

Background:

Recent extensive comparisons with the experimental data by Professor Michael Collins from the University of Ontario and the author of the Modified Compression Field Theory for shear design of concrete members, indicate that eliminating the term 0.5Cotq did not result in loss of accuracy.

The multiplier of 0.5Cotq in the numerator of equations 1, 2 and 3 requires assuming a value for the angle q and often requires performing several iterations. The revised form of equations 1, 2 and 3 eliminates the need for q angle iterations. The revised flow chart indicates the elimination of q angle iterations.
