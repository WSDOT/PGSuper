References - Lateral Stability for Lifting and Hauling Analisis

All calculations which determine factors of safety and stresses during handling and hauling of girders in PGSuper are based on methods described in the following papers:

New Deep WSDOT Standard Sections Extend Spans of Prestressed Concrete Girders

Stephen J. Seguirant, P.E.

PCI Journal, July-August 1998 Edition

Background material for the above paper may be found in both papers listed below. It is highly recommended that you obtain all three papers for complete coverage of this topic.

Lateral Stability of Long Prestressed Concrete Beams - Part 2

Robert F. Mast, P.E.

PCI Journal, January-February 1993 Edition

Lateral Stability of Long Prestressed Concrete Beams - Part 1

Robert F. Mast, P.E.

PCI Journal, January-February 1989 Edition

Another earlier, but excellent reference for Handling and Shipping issues is:

Handling and Shipping of Long Span Bridge Beams

George Laszlo, P.E. and Richard R. Imper, P.E.

PCI Journal, November-December 1987 Edition
