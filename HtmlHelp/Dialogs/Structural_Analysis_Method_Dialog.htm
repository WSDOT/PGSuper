Structural Analysis Method Dialog

PGSuper supports three methods of structural analysis.

Simple Span

Girders are assumed to be simple spans regardless of the connection boundary conditions

Simple Spans made Continuous

Girders are treated as simple spans until they are made continuous. Continuity can be achieved before or after deck placement as specified in the connection boundary conditions.

Envelope of Simple Span and Simple Spans made Continuous

Both of the previous analysis methods are used and their results enveloped. The controlling values are used.

Using the drop down list, select a method of structural analysis.

Note: The connection boundary conditions are defined as part of a bridge pier. See Chapter 3 for details.
