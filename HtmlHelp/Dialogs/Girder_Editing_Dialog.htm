Girder Editing Dialog

The Girder Editing Dialog allows you to enter information about a specific girder.

Tip: You can quickly copy the properties of one girder to the other girders by using the Copy Girder Properties dialog.

Once you have selected the girder you wish to edit you can visit the following tabs:

  • Strand Parameters

  • Debonding

  • Girder Materials

  • Longitudinal Reinforcement

  • Transverse Reinforcement

  • Lifting and Shipping

  • Condition and Rating

Tip: Double-Click on a girder in any view to bring up this dialog.
