Load Modifiers

Enter the values for Load Modifiers (h factors) for Ductility, Importance, and Redundancy for your bridge. Refer to Section 1.3.2.1 of the LRFD Specifications for more information.

Ductility

Select whether your structure is Non-Ductile, of Conventional Design, or has Enhanced Ductility. Enter the ductility factor hD if you selected Non-Ductile or Enhanced Ductility. This value applies to the Strength Limit State only. All other limit states have hD=1.0

Redundancy

Select whether your structure is Non-Redundant, of Conventional Level of Redundancy, or has Enhanced Redundancy. Enter the factor hI if you selected Non-Redundant or Enhanced Redundancy. This value applies to the Strength Limit State only. All other limit states have hR =1.0

Importance

Select whether your structure is Important, of Typical Importance, or is Not Important (yeah right). Enter the factor hI if you selected Important or Not Important. This value applies to the Strength Limit State only. All other limit states have hI =1.0
