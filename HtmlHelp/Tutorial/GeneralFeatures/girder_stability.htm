Girder Stability

As girders become longer, lifting and hauling them becomes more and more challenging. In fact, instability from lifting and handling often become the controlling factors when designing beams over 100 feet in length. PGSuper performs in-depth analyses to check criteria for lifting and hauling (See the Theoretical Manual for details).

To help designers better understand how the factors of safety against cracking, girder failure, and truck rollover are effected by the location of the lifting device and truck support locations, PGSuper has a Girder Stability View.

Viewing Girder Stability Graphs

1. Select View | Girder Stability.

2. Use the control bar at the top of the Girder Stability window to view lifting and hauling graphs for the girders in the current project. Go ahead and select some graphics now.
