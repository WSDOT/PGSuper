Wrapping Up

This concludes the general PGSuper tutorial. This tutorial covered the basic steps for creating a PGSuper Project, editing the bridge model, editing girders, performing girder designs, and reviewing analysis results. However, the tutorial only scratches the surface of PGSuper's many options and capabilities for designing precast girder bridges. Now you are ready to model your own precast-prestressed girder bridges with PGSuper. The best way to learn all of PGSuper's options is to consult the User's Guide.
