Pier Details - General

Station

Enter the pier station. The location of the piers connect the bridge to the alignment.

Use the drop down list to specify how the bridge configuration is to be adjusted when this pier is moved from one station to another.

Orientation

Enter the orientation of the pier. The orientation may be entered as the keyword "NORMAL", a bearing of the form N|S dd mm ss.s E|W, or a skew angle of the form dd mm ss.s L|R.

Examples

Normal to alignment

NORMAL

Bearing

N 45 34 43.2 E

Skew Angle

11 25 36.4 R
