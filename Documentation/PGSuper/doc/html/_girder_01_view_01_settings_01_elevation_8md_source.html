1 Elevation {#ug_dialogs_girder_View_settings_elevation}
2 ==============================================
3 Customize the girder elevation view by enabling or disabling the various view attributes.
4 
5 Item | Description
6 -----|---------
7 Show Strands | When checked, the prestressing strands are shown
8 Show Longitudinal Reinforcement | When checked, the longitudinal reinforcement is shown
9 Show Transverse Reinforcement (Stirrups) | When checked, the transverse reinforcement (stirrups) are shown
10 Show C.G. of Prestress Force | When checked, the center of gravity of the prestressing force is show with a red and white dashed line.
11 Show Dimensions | When checked, the longitudinal girder dimensions are shown
12 Schematic | When checked, the girder is drawn as a schematic, otherwise it is drawn with the height and width in the correct proportions.
13 Show User-Defined Loads | When checked, user-defined loads for the selected girder/stage are shown in the view. You may double-click on the loads to edit them.
14 Show Legend | When checked, the legend for user-defined loads is shown.
