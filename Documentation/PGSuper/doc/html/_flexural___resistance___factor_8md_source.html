1 Flexural Resistance Factor {#tg_flexural_resistance_factor}
2 ======================================
3 A generalized version of the flexure resistance factor defined in AASHTO LRFD 5.5.4.2.1 is used in this software. The resistance factor is a linear interpolation between the resistance factors for compression controlled and tension controlled sections. A linear interpolation is also made for partial prestress ratio between reinforced concrete and prestressed sections.
