22 Girder Concrete Properties
23 --------------------------
24 Define the concrete properties.
25 
26 Item | Description
27 -----|-----------
28 f'ci | Enter the concrete release strength
29 Eci | When checked, enter the modulus of elasticity at release. Otherwise, the modulus of elasticity will be computed by the AASHTO equation.
30 f'c | Enter the design concrete strength
31 Ec | When checked, enter the design modulus of elasticity. Otherwise, the modulus of elasticity will be computed by the AASHTO equation.
32 [More Properties...] | Press to define detailed concrete properties or select a pre-defined concrete from the library
33 
34 Fillet
35 ------
36 Use the combo box to control how fillets are defined for the bridge. Enter the fillet value for this girder if unique fillets are defined per girder.

41 
42 Item | Description
