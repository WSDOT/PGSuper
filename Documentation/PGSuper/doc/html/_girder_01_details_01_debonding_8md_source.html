5 Item | Description
6 -----|--------------
7 Strand Grid | This grid lists all of the strands modeled in the girder. Debonding and strand extension information is defined in this grid. See below for more information.
8 Symmetrical Debonding | When checked, debonding is the same at both ends of the girder.
9 
10 ### Strand Grid ###
11 The strand grid lists all of the strands modeled in the girder. Debonding and strand extension information is defined in this grid.
12 
13 Item | Description
14 -----|-----------
15 Strand # | Identifies a pair of strands, one on either side of the girder centerline. Refer to the girder image for strand location.
16 Debond | Check this box to model a strand as debonded
17 Debond Length Both Ends | Used for Symmetrical Debonding. Enter the debond length at both ends of the girder
18 Debond Length Left End | Enter the debond length at the left end of the girder
19 Debond Length Right End | Enter the debond length at the right end of the girder
20 Extend Left | Check this box to model a strand as extended at the left end of the girder
21 Extend Right | Check this box to model a strand as extended at the right end of the girder
22 
23 > NOTE: Strands in rows without a check box in the Debond column cannot be debonded because they were not designated as "debond-able" in the girder definition.
24 
25 > NOTE: Extended strands are considered to be fully developed along their entire length at the strength limit states.
26 
27 > NOTE: Extended strands are only available if the "Allow Strand Extensions" option is enabled in the Project Criteria.
