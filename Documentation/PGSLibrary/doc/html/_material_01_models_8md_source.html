1 Time-Dependent Material Models {#tg_time_dependent_material_models}
2 ======================================
3 Time-dependent material properties are computed using the AASHTO LRFD, ACI 209R-92, and CEB-FIP 1990 models.
4 
5 AASHTO LRFD
6 --------------------
7 
8 ### Compressive Strength ###
9 AASHTO does not define a time-dependent model for concrete compressive strength. The ACI 209R-92 model is used.
10 
11 ### Modulus of Elasticity ###
12 Modulus of elasticity is defined by a modified version of LRFD Equation 5.4.2.4-1. See @ref tg_concrete_properties for details.
13 
14 ### Shrinkage Strain ###
15 Shrinkage strain is defined by a modified version of LRFD Equation 5.4.2.3.3-1. See @ref tg_concrete_properties for details.
16 
17 ### Creep Coefficient ###
18 Creep coefficient is defined by a modified version of LRFD Equation 5.4.2.3.2-1. See @ref tg_concrete_properties for details.
19 
20 ### Relaxation of Prestressing Steel ###
21 AASHTO does not define an intrinsic model for strand relaxation. The ACI 209R-92 model is used.
22

46 ### Shrinkage Strain ###
47 Shrinkage strain is computed by ACI 209R-92 Equations 2-7, 2-9, 2-10, 2-15, 2-16, and 2-22. Here we present the shrinkage strain as an ultimate shrinkage value multiplied by modification factors.
48 ![](ACI_Shrinkage.png)

50 #### Time Modification Factor ####
51 ![](ACI_Shrinkage_Time_Factor.png)
52 f = 35 for moist cured concrete (ACI Eqn. 2-9) and 55 for steam cured concrete (ACI Eqn. 2-10). <br>
53 t = shrinkage duration
