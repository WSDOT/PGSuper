1 Diaphragms {#ug_library_dialogs_girder_diaphragms}
2 ==============================================
3 The location of intermediate diaphragms are defined by a set of rules based on span length. Intermediate diaphragms are created in teh bridge model for all applicable rules.
4 
5 The loads due to intermediate diaphragms are included in the dead load analysis. The concrete for intermediate diaphragms is assumed to be the same as that used for the deck.
6 
7 Intermediate Diaphragm Layout Rules
8 -----------------------------------
9 The diaphragm layout rules are listed in the grid. Use the [Add], [Edit], and [Delete] buttons to manage the rules. Double-clicking the mouse on a row will open the rule for editing.
10 
11 > TIP: It is wise to give the rules names that describe what the rule does for a given span range.
12 
13 
14 Example Diaphragm Layout Rules
15 ------------------------------
16 * Span length between 0 and 40 feet - No intermediate diaphragms
17 * 40 to 80 feet - 1 diaphragm at mid-span
18 * 80 to 120 feet - 2 diaphragms at third points
19 * greater than 120 feet (input as 120 to 99999 feet) - 3 diaphragms at quarter points
