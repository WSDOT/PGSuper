1 Features and Capabilities {#ug_introduction_features_and_capabilities}
2 ==============================================
3 PGSplice has an extensive list of features and capabilities. Here we give you the highlights. You'll learn more about the features and capabilities as your read this User Guide and the @ref tutorials.

5 Features
6 ---------
7 PGSplice has an extensive list of features. Here is a short list:
8 * Specification compliance checking for the AASHTO LRFD Bridge Design Specifications
9 * Load Rating, including automatic load posting analysis, for the AASHTO Manual for Bridge Evaluation
10 * Customizable project criteria including overrides and deviations from AASHTO provisions
11 * *Bridge-centric* user interface
12 * Detailed, graphically enhanced reports
13 * Wide variety of graphical results
14 * Customizable for all state departments of transportation
15 * Girder stability analysis for lifting and hauling
16 
17 Capabilities
18 -------------------
19 A wide variety of bridge configurations can be modeled. Here is a short list of the modeling capabilities:
20 
21 * Complex alignments including horizontal curves with transition spirals, vertical curves, and superelevation transitions.
22 * Modeling of spliced girder bridges with variable depth cantilever pier segments, drop-in field segments supported by strongback hangers, temporary erection towers, and multi-stage post-tensioning
23 * Multiple span bridges with simple, integral, and continuous boundary conditions
24 * Physical models of interior piers
25 * Complex framing plans including flared (non-parallel) girders and different number and type of girder in each span
26 * Sidewalks and pedestrian live loads
27 * User defined vehicles
28 * Non-linear time-step analysis using time-dependent material models from AASHTO LRFD, ACI 209, and CEB-FIP.
