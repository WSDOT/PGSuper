Welcome to TOGATM

TOGA (TxDOT Optional Girder Analysis), is a plug-in application for the PGSuper Program. TOGA is used to support the precast girder design value engineering process at the Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT) Bridge Division.

 

PGSuper and TOGA are software tools jointly developed by the Washington State and Texas Departments of Transportation and are members of the Alternate Route Project.

 

PGSuper and TOGA are licensed under the terms and conditions of the Alternate Route Open Source License. Please read this license carefully before using this software.

Getting Started

TOGA documentation consists of two manuals to help you use, manage, and understand this state of the art software.  The User's Guide provides in-depth coverage of the software's features and functionality. The Theoretical Manual details how TOGA is performing its engineering tasks.
